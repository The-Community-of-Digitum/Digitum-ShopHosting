# 🥖 Official Deep‑Fried Bread (DFB) Recipe — Year 1
*For National Deep‑Fried Bread Day in The Republic of Digitum*

## 🧾 Ingredients
- 2 cups all‑purpose flour  
- 2 tbsp sugar  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 tbsp baking powder  
- ¾ cup warm water  
- 1 tbsp oil/ghee (for the dough)  
- Neutral frying oil/liquid ghee (for deep frying)

Optional toppings:
- Powdered sugar  
- Cinnamon sugar  
- Honey  
- Coffee syrup  
- Butter  

## 🥣 Instructions

### 1. Make the Dough
1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.  
2. Add warm water and 1 tbsp oil/ghee.  
3. Stir until a soft dough forms.  
4. Knead gently for 1–2 minutes.  
5. Rest for 10 minutes.

### 2. Shape the Bread
1. Tear off small handfuls of dough.  
2. Flatten into rough circles or ovals.  
3. Imperfect shapes are encouraged — these are “Forkt Folds.”

### 3. Fry
1. Heat oil or liquid ghee to **350°F / 175°C**.  
2. Place dough pieces into the hot fat.  
3. Fry until golden, puffed, and crisp at the edges.  
4. Flip once.  
5. Drain on paper towels.

### 4. Serve
Top with:
- powdered sugar  
- cinnamon sugar  
- honey  
- or enjoy plain (the classic Digitum way)

Serve warm. Share freely. Celebrate loudly.
